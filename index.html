About Me

Hello, I’m Sajitha. I live in the beautiful village of Sigiriya and have been a tuk-tuk driver here for over 25 years. With my deep knowledge of Sigiriya’s vibrant culture, scenic landscapes, and the iconic Rock Fortress, I provide travelers with unique and authentic experiences. Join me to explore hidden paths, discover local stories, and immerse yourself in the heart of Sigiriya.
